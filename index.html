the story

Since I was a child, I wanted to be a matematician. Use huge computers and program in FORTRAN. But I grown up and met JavaScript - the contestation of programming languages. A new era began.

I am an infinite learner, tester, writer. You will often find me digging internet searching for new apps, libraries or frameworks to read about and test. I am also a fan of FOSS, the idea, the software, the community. All the good things that makes me belive in people.

I use VSCode for most of my jobs because it's super handy but I am also learning and configuring own Vim setup. I love experiments and I heard a lot of good things about how great this tool can be. In the past I used to work pretty much in Adobe CS (PS, Illustrator, After Effects) but currently my main and beloved graphic tool is Figma. It's neat for sketches and prototypes that sometimes I need to prepare.

I like to help others, really, so I started a World Wide Developers project. A small group of enthusiasts helping non-profit organizations with admin and development tasks that they cannot afford by themselves.

history

Briefly, here are the past 10 years of my being as a banker, analyst, freelancer, designer and finally as a web developer. So many different things ahead, great experiences, and a lot of knowledge. A lucky beggar.

2019
I joined swiss technology company - zetcom. It has a great and awarded software for managing art collections for museums and galleries. I joined to lead a project which is going to upgrade the company stack when it comes to publication online (some art collections are being publicly available to view). I am also a support person for the company REST API and change requests that are comming for the legacy Java application. Besides that, I am running current company website and implementing a technology change from WordPress to Next.js stack.
2016
That year bringed a great turn around in my life, in many aspects. My son was born and I left the banking area for the full-time web designer/developer job. Little scared, very happy. I started in a danish FinTech company - Flex Funding. The company has crowdlending marketplace application as a main product and few innovative ideas in the area of financing local businesses. I was the all-in-one for the front of company digital being. I was working on branding materials for print and web, design and implementation of company website, some marketing tasks like newsletters, A/B tests, playing with Google Tag Manager, HotJar or HubSpot.
2012
During my time as a banker I started experiments with graphic design. I spent nights on polishing Photoshop and Illustrator skills. Did few animations in Flash, made some banners in After Effects and even (award winning) short movie mastered entirely in Premiere. Also, back in then I met fractals and variety of apps that allow me to render this beautiful piece of math. I am rendering them till today. After some time of playing with random graphics I was asked to prepare a concept for a website. I think that this was a very beginning of the way to full time web developer.
2008
I have started as an analyst in a big financial institution - british RBS. This was my first real job and a great experience. I had to investigate billion dollar payments, link connections between involved parties, fix broken elements, and count every minute as a loss. After few years I moved to another bank - Citi - to gain some new skills, but back in then I already knew that this is not my way. Well, it was, but in a different manner - in the second place I met my wonderful wife.
